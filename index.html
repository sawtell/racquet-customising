Racquet matching

Many players I know make the decision to buy at least two racquets. This is so if they break a string while playing they can switch to an identical racquet. The truth is that the chance of two racquets being the same is actually very slim due to the tollarances (or lack thereof) that manufacturers produce tennis racquets to. This means that two racquets of the same model can be vastly different.

You may have noticed this yourself and found that you have a favourite, or that one doesn't feel quite like the others.

What's involved

As part of the racquet matching service I will determine the weight, balance and swingweight of the racquets and make sure that they are all the same. If you have a favourite racquet then I can use that as a target to match the others.

Limitations

Due the nature of racquet customising it is very easy to add weight but very difficult to remove weight. Because of this there may be scenarios when matching racquets that cannot be achieved.
